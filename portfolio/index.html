About Me

Born in Monterrey Mexico, I did study a bachelor in Industrial and Systems Engineering and a MA in Software and Information Technologies. At the 12 years I had my first contact with programming (qBasic), the only idea of create virtually whatever I could imagine, captured me right away.

In 2001 a I has my first job as a Full Stack Web and Desktop application developer working with Java, JSP, Struts, Oracle 10i and Progress for an open source ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning) system kown as Compiere. For the year 2006 worked with SCADA systems with ANSI C and C++. In most recent years I been working on HMI (Human Machine Interface) applications for a Furnace Regulation System with Microsoft .Net technologies.

But not everything in life is work; I love music! So I play as a DJ in free time, I like go out to skate and go to see shows. Did I said I also like movies? ... Well I like it!.
